Biohacking

Biohacking is when you make small changes in your life, that allow you to use your body to it's fullest potentional. There is a wide range of changes you can implement into your everyday life.

You could go extreme, like this person who spent over $200k on biohacking. Extreme Biohacking

Or doing something as simple as prioriting sleep and taking vitamins that your body might be lacking.

It is totally up to you.
