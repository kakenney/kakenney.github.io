Seeking Knowledge



I LOVE Learning!!!

There is nothing better.

I am always learning something new.

It can be a new skill, like crocheting, painting, or woodworking.

It could be something like just digging deeper into something I might already know, like cooking, exercising, or better ways to cut my dog's hair.

It could also be a totally different industry. I have worked in the service industry, the pharmaceutical world, now managing a Car Wash and moving into Cyber Security.

I would highly encourage everyone to seek out knowledge. I think it keeps you young and helps you grow as a person in more ways than just learning a new skill
