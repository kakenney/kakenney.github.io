Giving Back

Giving back… What does that mean?

Well, I don’t know that there is one answer to that question. Giving back can mean so many things to different people. I think you also must know your capacity and abilities.

For some, that can be having positive interactions with those to come in contact with every day. For some, that can be being a mentor to someone in need. For some, that can be volunteering your time.

For some, that can be donating to a cause.

I think the most important thing is being a good and kind person, because you never know what another is going through and the simplest things can have a huge impact on their lives.

If you can find your “why” and spread that through your life you are bound to bring the same passion to others. Spread what you love.



How I like to give back:

Hurricanes Sled Hockey is a non profit orginization committed to seeing that everyone can play hockey.

They have a youth and adult team, both full of amazing individuals.

My husband is one of the founding members. I have been helping in tha background since it started, since we have 2 young kids. Now that the kids have gotten a bit older I have been able to take a more active role with the team. I just started helping with coaching this season and it is one of the most rewarding things I have done. I get to work with athletes that are just starting out and some that might be in the olympics one day.

It is so much more then a game...Hockey Changes Lives!
